About Us

Who are we?

Founded in May 2022, Clay and Fire Jewellery is a handmade jewellery business, focusing on creating jewellery that is both perfect for everyday wear but can also be added to transition from day to night.

The majority of our jewellery is created using polymer clay. This is due to its durability, vibrancy and lightweight feel. Even our larger pieces, especially earrings, can be worn all day without irritation.

Our Values

Sustainability

We value the planet, that's why we create our pieces as they are ordered, ensuring we use only the required materials. We also ensure all of our packaging is recyclable and does not contain plastic.

We endeavour to continuously improve our impact on the environment and develop our sustainability strategies to look after the world whilst doing what we love - making jewellery that our customers love and wear on a regular basis.

Our Customers

We wouldn't be here without our amazing customers, therefore, we believe their experience of the brand to be of the upmost importance. All of our products are made with care and attention to detail to ensure they are both durable and made to a high quality. We believe in value before profit and keep that in mind when creating our designs.

Cost-conscious

We would love our designs to be accessible to everyone, therefore there is a wide selection of pieces in varied price ranges to accommodate a variety of shoppers without compromising on the quality of our products.
